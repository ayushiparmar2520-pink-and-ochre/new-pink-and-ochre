## 📝 Typography Enhancements

### Previous Fonts
- Display: Playfair Display (serif)
- Body: Inter (sans-serif)

### New Font System ✅
| Font | Usage | Weight |
|------|-------|--------|
| **Playfair Display** | Headlines & Display | 700, 800 |
| **Lora** | Body text & paragraphs | 400, 500, 600, 700 |
| **Inter** | Navigation, buttons, labels | 400, 500, 600, 700 |

**Why This Change?**
- **Lora** (serif) for body text creates an elegant, literary feel
- Better readability and premium appearance
- More sophisticated than sans-serif body text
- Matches the aesthetic of high-end lifestyle publications

### Improved Letter Spacing
- Wide letter spacing (0.15em) on uppercase labels
- Wider spacing (0.25em) on category tags and buttons
- Creates the "breathing room" seen in luxury brand websites

## 🎯 Key Design Principles Applied

### 1. **Whitespace & Breathing Room**
- Generous padding and margins
- Uncluttered layouts
- Emphasis on quality over quantity

### 2. **Typography Hierarchy**
- Large, elegant headings
- Readable body text
- Clear visual distinction

### 3. **Color Restraint**
- Neutral palette with gold accents
- Gold used strategically for hover states
- Sophisticated, timeless feel

### 4. **Minimal Decoration**
- No heavy shadows
- Subtle borders
- Clean, square elements (2px radius)

### 5. **Intentional Motion**
- Smooth transitions (300-700ms)
- Subtle zoom effects
- No unnecessary animations
